Natural & Floral Attars

"The Heart of the Garden, Bottled." Experience the unadulterated essence of nature with The Triple Acts Floral Collection. Unlike mass-produced perfumes, our natural attars are distilled from hand-picked blossoms to capture the true "soul" of the flower. Each drop offers a concentrated, alcohol-free journey through sun-drenched gardens and morning dew. Whether you seek the romantic allure of a rose or the intoxicating mystery of jasmine, our floral range provides a soft, lingering trail that remains true to its botanical roots.

1. Rose Attar (The Timeless Romance):
A tribute to the "Queen of Flowers," this attar captures the fresh, dewy scent of Damask roses at dawn. It is a balanced, elegant fragrance that radiates classic femininity and grace.

2. Cabbage Rose Attar (The Honeyed Bloom):
Distilled from the rare Rosa Centifolia, this scent is deeper and sweeter than a standard rose. It carries rich, honey-like undertones with a complex velvet finish that feels incredibly luxurious on the skin.

3. Jasmine Attar (The Midnight Soul):
An intense, intoxicating aroma that mirrors the scent of jasmine blooming under a full moon. It is an uplifting and sensual fragrance, blending creamy floral notes with a hint of green freshness.
